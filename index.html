INFO ABOUT ME

Hi all. I am Michelle Pisters, a Computer Science student at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. I have always been interested in technology and computers. I did my bachelor in Lifestyle Informatics at the VU. Here I learned that I wanted to do some more technical study, therefore I chose to do the computer science master. Also in my last year of my bachelors I started to work as JavaScript developer which was a great experience. Here I learned to make web applications. In the future I want to be a great developer, so I am learning some new programming languages and programming techniques to make this possible.
